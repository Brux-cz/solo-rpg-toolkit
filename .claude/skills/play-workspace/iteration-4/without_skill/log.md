## Summary of the Arc (Scenes 8-12)
Horacio's story arc across these 5 scenes moves from investigation to resolution to new crisis. In Scene 8, he catches saboteur Blazej Sipek but is badly wounded. Scene 9 is recovery and the beginning of Blazej's redemption -- a week of healing and cautious dialogue. Scene 10 finally gets the trio into the mine through a charming side entrance guarded by happy bees. Scene 11 brings the first successful smelting but also a new threat: Blazej's former allies are trying to get the authorities involved. Scene 12's trip to the city for a permit ends in crushing failure -- the councilor dismisses them entirely. The campaign ends on a cliffhanger: Horacio has iron, has a working forge, has allies, but faces legal doom from the city and a sick mayor who can't help. The next steps likely involve either curing Bertik (so he can issue local authorization), finding leverage over councilor Kopr, or dealing with Blazej's former helpers directly.
