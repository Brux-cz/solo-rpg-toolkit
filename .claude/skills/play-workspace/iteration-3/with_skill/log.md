## Story Arc Summary
Konrad Chalva, a displaced worm herder, rescued Sedmikraska from a bandit trap (Scene 2). Together they searched an attacked merchant cart and found supplies (Scene 3). On the road they encountered Kremilek, a bandit posing as a victim, but Sedmikraska recognized his disguise (Scene 4). They arrived in the frightened town of Bobulin where bandits had disrupted trade (Scene 5). Starosta Hroznys demanded proof before offering work (Scene 6). Konrad investigated the bandits' abandoned mill and found stolen gold and a map of their ambush routes, earning the starosta's trust (Scene 7). He now has a path to becoming Bobulin's guard, but the bandits remain at large.
