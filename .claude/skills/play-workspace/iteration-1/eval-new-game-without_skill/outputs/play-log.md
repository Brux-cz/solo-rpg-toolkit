- **Torch Usage**: The torch was narratively implied during the night watch but not tracked/consumed in the inventory.
- **Combat**: No combat occurred (the beetle was not dangerous).
- **NPC Wiki Cards**: The NPC wiki cards in the Svět tab were not updated with detailed information (descriptions, locations, relationships).
- **Weather Check**: The reminder to check weather was shown but not acted upon.
- **Gold**: The promised 10 ďobků payment was not added (would happen after completing the guard job).
